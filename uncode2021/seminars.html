Recordings, and references from the 2018 workshop

Seminar Overview: The seminars were intended to engage a diversity of stakeholders in discussions about key issues that affect the landscape of microbial systematics. Each one-hour seminar featured two 15-minute presentations, followed by 15 minutes of discussion in break out groups of 4-5 participants, and 15 minutes of reporting by the break out groups. To maximize productivity, all participants were strongly encouraged to carefully read the assigned papers and develop opinions and ideas for discussions prior to each seminar.

  • There is no single eukaryotic code and how we deal with sequence data - John Freudenstein (with help from Chris Lane)

Monday, October 15, @ 4 pm GMT: Do we need a new system?

Monday, October 22, @ 4 pm GMT: Efforts to scale and systematize taxonomy in the 21st century
