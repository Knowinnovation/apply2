Overview

The National Cancer Institute’s Center for Strategic Scientific Initiatives launched the Creative Collaborations Foundry in 2023 to spur innovative solutions to intractable problems in cancer control. The Foundry’s Innovation Labs aim to accelerate progress by creating opportunities to form new multidisciplinary collaborations and generate novel ideas.

The Challenge

These kinds of conflicting health messages are an inevitable result of the scientific process, and yet we know that conflicting health messages lead to confusion, mistrust, disengagement, and difficulty making health decisions. In part, these consequences of conflicting health messaging result from a failure to convey accurately the nature of information and scientific discovery, leaving many poorly prepared to interpret and use new information to make health decisions. New strategies are needed to prepare communicators and the public to deal with conflicting information when it inevitably arises.
